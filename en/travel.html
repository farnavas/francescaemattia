How to get there

There are three Airports in the area:
Milano Malpensa, Milano Bergamo, and Milano Linate.

You can reach Como by Train through Milan or directly from Switzerland.


The Bellagio area is well served by public transport:
Busses and Ferries are available.


Reach the venue from Bellagio, however, is quite complicated.

We therefore suggest to rent a car to get around more freely.


Taxis (incl. Uber, Bolt, ecc) are not available in the area.
