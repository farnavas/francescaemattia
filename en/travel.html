How to get there

There are three Airports in the region: Milano Malpensa, Milano Bergamo, and Milano Linate.

You can also reach Como by Train, from Milan or Switzerland.

The area offers public transport options, including Buses and Ferries.


Getting from Bellagio to the venue is challenging via public transport. We strongly recommend renting a car for flexibility and ease of travel.

Please note that taxis (including Uber and Bolt) are not available.


We're here to assist! Let us know your travel details, we'll help coordinate transportation and carpooling arrangements to ensure everyone arrives comfortably.
